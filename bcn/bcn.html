About Us

Black Coffee Network is a pan African youth network and organization. It is registered as a community-based organization in Nakuru County. Founded in 2017, it started with a few friends who shared a cup of coffee while discussing issues they faced. It has now grown into a network of young people across the county and beyond, using coffee as a means to bring people together.

Our Vision

An expanded youth network operating across the African continent and beyond, spreading activities to various high schools, campuses, and youth incubation centers.

Our Mission

To be a youth-led organization and network that spearheads real change in Africa by encouraging youth participation across all sectors of development and fostering a positive African spirit among the youth.
